## What is the Pilot Client?

The **BARS Pilot Client** is the bridge between your flight simulator and the BARS ecosystem. It dynamically injects airport lighting objects into your simulator, enabling controllers to manage realistic, real-time airport lighting during your flights.

### How It Works

The Pilot Client operates through a seamless three-step process:

1. **Receives Real-Time State** — The client connects to the BARS Core server and continuously receives live lighting state updates from active controllers
2. **Loads Your Scenery Package** — Based on your configuration, it loads the scenery package positions for your current airport
3. **Spawns and Controls Lights** — Using BARS contribution data, the client places airport lights at precise positions in your simulator. Controllers can then adjust these lights in real-time through VatSys or Euroscope, and you'll see the changes instantly in your cockpit

<Tip>
Since the Pilot Client communicates directly with the BARS Core server (which connects to controller plugins), you get real-time lighting changes without any delay or manual updates.
</Tip>
