# BARS vatSys Plugin

The BARS vatSys plugin brings realistic aerodrome control capabilities to virtual air traffic control, simulating the stop bar systems used at real-world airports to enhance runway safety and operational realism.

## What are Stop Bars?

In real-world aviation, stop bars are rows of illuminated red lights embedded in taxiways at runway intersections. They serve as a critical safety system that prevents aircraft from inadvertently entering active runways without explicit clearance from air traffic control.

<Info>
  The BARS plugin synchronizes stop bar status between controllers using vatSys
  and pilots using the BARS client, creating a seamless and realistic ground
  control experience.
</Info>

## Compatible Airports

The BARS plugin currently supports major international airports with implemented stop bar systems. For a complete list of compatible airports, visit the [BARS Website](https://stopbars.com).

<Tip>
  If your preferred airport isn't currently supported, you can request
  implementation through the BARS Discord community or GitHub repository.
</Tip>

## Quick Start

<Steps>
<Step title="Create Account">
  Sign up at [stopbars.com](https://stopbars.com) and obtain your authentication token
</Step>

<Step title="Download Plugin">
  Install the vatSys plugin from the BARS website downloads section
</Step>

<Step title="Configure Token">
  Enter your token in vatSys through Windows > BARS Settings
</Step>

<Step title="Claim Airport">
  Select your active runways and begin controlling stop bars
</Step>
</Steps>

Ready to get started? Continue to the [Installation Guide](/vatsys/installation) for detailed setup instructions.
