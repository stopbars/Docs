After testing and verifying your contribution through the XML Preview, you're ready to submit. This page covers the final details, submission process, and how to manage your contributions.

## Contribution Details

From the test page, proceed to the next step. Here on the Details page, you provide information about your scenery package and contribution.

<Steps>
<Step title="Enter your scenery package name">
  Enter the name of the scenery package your contribution is built for. For example, if you're contributing for an iniBuilds scenery, the name would be **iniBuilds**.

  Below the input field, a list of the four most popular scenery packages are available as quick-select options. If your scenery package name isn't listed, type it directly into the input field.
</Step>

<Step title="Select your simulator">
  Choose the simulator this contribution is built for - Microsoft Flight Simulator 2020 or 2024.
</Step>

<Step title="Add notes">
  Add any additional notes that may be important for the review process. These notes are visible to reviewers during the process and allow you to highlight concerns or provide context the review team may not be aware of.

  <Note>
  If you're re-contributing to an airport that already has an existing contribution - for example, because you've fixed lighting positions or added missing remove areas - mention this in your notes so the review team has context.
  </Note>
</Step>

<Step title="Confirm the Contribution Acknowledgement">
  Read and confirm the Contribution Acknowledgement. By submitting, you confirm that:

  - You have followed the contribution guidelines
  - Your submission has been tested and works correctly
  - This work is your own
</Step>
</Steps>

## Submit Your Contribution

Before clicking **Submit**, double-check that your scenery package name and simulator selection are correct, review and add any notes required.

<Warning>
Ensure your scenery package name and simulator are set correctly before submitting.
</Warning>

Once you click **Submit**, your contribution is automatically assigned for review.

## Review Process

After submission, the BARS team will review your contribution. There are two possible outcomes:

| Outcome | What Happens |
|---------|-------------|
| **Approved** | Your contribution is accepted and immediately available for use by pilots. |
| **Rejected** | Your contribution is not accepted. A comment explaining the reason for rejection will be provided. |

If your contribution is rejected and you are unsure why, or wish to dispute the decision, join the [BARS Discord server](https://stopbars.com/discord) and contact staff with your contribution ID.

<Note>
If you need to remove an approved contribution from BARS, contact the team at **support@stopbars.com** with your contribution ID and reason for removal.
</Note>

## Display Name

Your name is publicly visible on the Contribution Dashboard to all visitors. You can control how your name appears publicly across BARS.

Navigate to your [account settings](https://preview.stopbars.com/account) and under **Preferred Display Name Mode**, select one of:

- **First Name**
- **First Name + Last Initial**
- **VATSIM CID**

Once updated, your name will be updated on the Contribution Dashboard to reflect your chosen display preference.

## Top Contributors

The **Top Contributors** leaderboard on the Contribution Dashboard recognises users who submit the most contributions. It's our way of saying thank you and giving recognition to the community members who go above and beyond in expanding airport support.
