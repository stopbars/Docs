---

Before submitting your contribution, you should export your scenery XML and test it through the Contribution Tester. This step allows you to visually verify your work and catch any issues before the review process.

## Pre-Export Checklist

Before exporting, perform one final check over your contribution:

- All lighting polygon positions are accurate to your scenery
- Every polygon is named with the correct BARS ID
- Remove areas cover all default scenery lights that BARS will replace
- No duplicate or missing polygons

<Info>
Taking the time to verify your work before exporting will save time during the review process. Contributions with incorrect BARS IDs or missing remove areas will be rejected.
</Info>

### Verify Remove Areas

Within the **Map Settings** on the XML Preview, you can enable **Toggle Remove Areas**. This filters the preview to show only your remove areas, allowing you to confirm they cover all relevant default scenery lighting.

## What to Look For

When reviewing the XML Preview, check for:

- **Polygon positions** - Lighting polygons should align accurately with taxiway and runway positions
- **BARS ID accuracy** - Verify that the preview shows the correct light types in the correct locations
- **Remove area coverage** - Ensure all default scenery lights within your contribution area are covered by remove areas
- **Missing objects** - Confirm you haven't missed any lighting objects from the airport map

If you identify any issues, return to the simulator, make corrections, re-export your XML.
